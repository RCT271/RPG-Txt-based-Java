Here are the commands that you can attach to your scene's commands (or the choices that the user can make for every scene):



up
down
left
right

These are the default commands. It will move your current scene to that direction relative to the scene map that you have declared.



local + {name of your local scene}

This will take your scene to a local scene that you have specified.
A local scene is a scene that is not present in scene map



leave

This will exit you from the local scene and bring you back to the previous scene from the scene map



{name of a different area}

This will change your area and change your scene to the default scene of the area that you have created



callback
This will invoke the callback that you set on the scene with the same index from the command
